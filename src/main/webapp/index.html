Why my future classroom?

Since I was a kid, I always like gaming and I wondered more than once why studying is not as interesting as gaming？

Until one day I got my first VR headset and it's like entering another word. In this world, you can see places you haven't been to ;
you can look through the complexities of science;
you can create things without limit,
like using tilt brush and medium to make 3D sculptures then 3d print them.

I can't wait to share it with my friends and classmates. It Really enlightens my imagination and open my vision to a bigger world.
Also it makes me think about educational equality, as people who got limited access to modern tools can now have another choice.

Welcome to my future classroom！
