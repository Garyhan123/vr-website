From: https://www.vrcircle.com/insomniac-games-reclaim-your-world-in-vr/

 

From the creators or Edge of Nowhere and The Unspoken, Insomniac Games is teasing a new video for a new game with more information coming June 7, 2018. Insomniac games claim the new game will "Establish a sense of wonder" and "you'll be able to go where ever you want."

 

As yet, we do not know which platform the game will support, or even the name of the game. However, since the video has an Oculus logo, it will almost certainly launch on the Oculus Rift. Remember that both The Unspoken and Edge of Nowhere are Oculus exclusives.

Until June 7, we will wait to see if we will “Reclaim your world.”
