It  was a Friday afternoon when the scanning of our school campus takes place.

a document was first set up, awaiting for input of scans: each scan follows a guide route for our school and are about 1 M apart from another take.

Moving the scanning device step by step and sketching more of the surrounding feels very accomplished.

The overall takes were then submitted into a computer, specially used to process the details and render the environment.

 

Through a VR tour, students can preview the school anywhere, at anytime.

 

When I showed my classmates and teachers about this project, they were surprised at the great details and viewing as if walking in the school.

Quoting from my classmate: “Even though I knew I am not in the campus, it feels so real of the surrounding that I am there.”
